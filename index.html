Research Projects & Publications

Research Projects

Ph.D. Thesis: Sustainable Remediation of Infectious Liquid Medical Waste through Nanomaterial-Powered Photocatalysis

Developed Ag-, Cu-, Ag-Cu co-doped ZnO and Pd-doped Ga₂O₃ nanophotocatalysts using the sol-gel method for efficient degradation of liquid biomedical waste. The Ag-Cu-ZnO nanocomposite demonstrated superior photocatalytic and antimicrobial efficiency.

Postgraduate Project: Analysis of Tissue Compositional Changes by Laser Multiprobe Reflectance Imaging Technique

Used laser backscattering and reflectance imaging to detect structural and compositional changes in tissue phantoms and human forearms.
